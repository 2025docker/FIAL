Financial Insights Guide

How Your KPIs Are Calculated

Understanding your financial metrics

External money earned (salary, freelance, business). NOT asset sales.
Income = Σ type = 'income'
Note: Selling your own assets does NOT increase Income. It's asset conversion (asset → cash).
All money spent on consumables and daily needs.
Expenses = Σ all transactions with type = 'expense'
Current value of purchased assets + DCA value. DCA counts as asset when invested.
Assets = Σ Asset Buys - Σ Asset Sells + DCA
Available cash after expenses + asset sales - DCA invest + DCA withdraw - locked.
Balance = Income - Expenses - Asset Buys + Asset Sells - DCA Invest + DCA Withdraw - Locked
Auto-allocated savings from income (configurable rate).
Locked = Σ income.lockedAmount (autoSaveRate × income)
Net invested capital via Dollar Cost Averaging. DCA adds to Assets when invested, removes when withdrawn.
Total DCA = Σ DCA Invest - Σ DCA Withdraw
Average income per active day (days with transactions).
Daily Avg = Income / Active Days
Average income per active month.
Monthly Avg = Income / Active Months
Total savings percentage: (Balance + Locked Savings) / Income
Savings Rate = (Balance + Locked) / Income × 100
What percentage of your capital is in assets (including DCA).
Asset Ratio = (Assets / Capital) × 100
Your true net worth: Balance + Locked + Assets (DCA included).
Capital = Balance + Locked + Assets
